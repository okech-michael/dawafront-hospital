How We Attend to You

Our streamlined patient journey ensures you receive timely, thorough, and compassionate care from arrival to follow-up.

01

Reception & Registration

Upon arrival, our friendly reception team greets you and registers your details including personal information, insurance coverage, and the reason for your visit. New patients are issued a unique patient file number for continuity of care. We aim to complete registration within 5 minutes.

Consultation with a Doctor

You will be seen by one of our qualified physicians in a private consultation room. The doctor takes a thorough history, performs a physical examination, and discusses your symptoms and concerns in detail. We ensure patients are fully informed about their condition and the proposed care plan.

Diagnosis & Investigations

Where necessary, the doctor will request laboratory tests, imaging (X-ray or ultrasound), or other investigations to support an accurate diagnosis. Our on-site laboratory and radiology services mean you get results quickly — most same-day — without having to travel elsewhere.

Treatment & Medication

Based on the diagnosis, the doctor prescribes a treatment plan tailored to your individual needs. Medications are dispensed directly from our pharmacy. For admitted patients, treatment includes nursing care, IV therapy, physiotherapy referral if needed, and daily review by the clinical team.

Discharge & Follow-Up

When you are ready to be discharged, our team provides a full discharge summary including diagnosis, treatments given, medications to take, and signs to watch for. Follow-up appointments are booked before you leave. We also offer a phone follow-up service for patients with chronic or complex conditions.

Our Commitment

Patient Rights & Our Values

Dignity & Respect

Every patient is treated with the utmost dignity and respect regardless of their background, condition, or ability to pay. Discrimination of any kind is not tolerated.

Privacy & Confidentiality

Your medical information is strictly confidential. Consultations are conducted privately, and records are securely stored in compliance with Kenyan health data regulations.

Right to Information

You have the right to know your diagnosis, treatment options, expected outcomes, and costs. Our doctors explain everything clearly and answer all your questions.

Informed Consent

No procedure is performed without your informed consent. We explain all risks, benefits, and alternatives before any treatment to ensure you can decide freely.

Feedback Welcome

We actively encourage patient feedback to improve our services. Complaint boxes, online forms, and direct communication channels are always available to you.

Affordable Care

We accept NHIF and multiple private insurance plans to make quality healthcare accessible to all. Flexible payment arrangements are available for those in need.
