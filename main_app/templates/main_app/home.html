About Us

A Trusted Healthcare Partner in Kisumu

Dawafront Healthcare Clinic is a recognized Level 3 Medical Clinic located in Lolwe Shopping Centre, Kisumu. We serve the Kondele Ward, Migosi Ward, and the broader Kisumu Central constituency with compassionate, accessible, and professional medical care.

Regulated by the Pharmacy & Poisons Board and fully operational since January 2024, we combine modern medical practices with a patient-first approach to deliver comprehensive healthcare for individuals and families.

Our Services

Comprehensive Medical Services

From routine check-ups to specialized care, Dawafront Healthcare offers a wide range of medical services for every age and condition.

Outpatient Care

Comprehensive consultations and treatments for patients who don't require overnight admission.

Inpatient Care

Full in-hospital care with round-the-clock nursing and medical supervision for admitted patients.

Emergency Services

Immediate medical attention for accidents, sudden illnesses, and critical conditions — available 24/7.

Maternity

Safe, supportive maternity care including antenatal, delivery, and postnatal services.

Pediatrics

Specialized healthcare for newborns, infants, children, and teenagers in a child-friendly environment.

Laboratory

Advanced diagnostic tests including blood work, urinalysis, malaria tests, and culture studies.

Radiology

Digital imaging services including X-rays and ultrasounds to support accurate diagnosis.

Pharmacy

Fully stocked pharmacy with qualified pharmacists providing prescriptions and medication advice.
